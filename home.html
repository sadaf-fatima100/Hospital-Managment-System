This slide showcases the short and long-term goals of the hospital. It includes vision: to become a leading hospital in the country and mission: to provide top care and cure.

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading hospital in the country, providing healthcare services of international standards and positively impacting the lives of the people we serve by delivering exceptional clinical care, ensuring superior quality, and demonstrating unwavering commitment.

Mission statement

  • To provide Hope, Care and Cure
  • Through research and technology integration, new knowledge and technology will be developed, shared, and applied in the delivery of patient care
  • To become the top location for academic and medical research
